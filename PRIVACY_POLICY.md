## Overview

Optical Tool is a business management application for optical shops. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how it is protected.

---

## Data We Collect

### Account Data
- Name and email address (via Google OAuth or email registration)
- Encrypted password credentials (managed by Supabase Auth)

### Business Data
- Optical prescriptions entered by the user (SPH, CYL, AXIS, ADD values)
- Invoice and billing records
- Customer names and contact details added by the shop owner
- Shop profile information (name, address, branding)

### Images
- Prescription photos uploaded by the user
- Images are compressed and stored securely in Supabase Storage

### Device Data
- No device identifiers, location data, or usage analytics are collected

---

## How We Use Your Data

- To provide core app functionality (prescriptions, invoices, analytics)
- To sync data across sessions via cloud storage
- To restore data after reinstallation or device change
- We **do not** sell, share, or monetize any user data

---

## Data Storage

- **Local storage:** Room Database on your device (offline-first)
- **Cloud storage:** Supabase (hosted on secure infrastructure)
- Data is tied to your account and is only accessible by you
